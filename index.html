ABOUT ME

I’m the Web Services Manager for RUSA and ASCLA, two divisions of the American Library Association. In this role I have a variety of responsibilities, including: developing and maintaining multiple websites, digital content and database management, and shaping digital marketing strategy. I'm also the Online Learning Coordinator.

Before joining the ALA, I worked in private and public education as a librarian, web developer, and programming/technology teacher. I was also lucky enough to spend four years co-organizing an annually sold-out TEDx conference.

I believe in serving my community, that details are vitally important, and that Firefly was the best television show ever created. When I'm not working, you'll probably find me in my garden where I grow both flowers and vegetables, admittedly with mixed results. I may be talking to the chickens, as well.
